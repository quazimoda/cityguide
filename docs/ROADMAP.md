# Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP informational city guide — done

The initial MVP includes local content files, city guide pages, search, contact and subscribe forms, sitemap, robots, and basic SEO.

## Phase 2: production readiness and CI — current PR

Add dependency locking, CI checks, deployment hygiene, site configuration, and documentation for predictable GitHub and Vercel deployments.

## Phase 3: content quality expansion

Improve guide depth, editorial quality, coverage, and internal linking.

## Phase 4: newsletter/email provider integration

Connect subscription and contact flows to a production email or newsletter provider.

## Phase 5: admin/content management

Introduce a content management workflow or admin surface when the local content file approach becomes limiting.

## Phase 6: monetization/partners/advertising

Evaluate partner listings, sponsorships, ads, and other revenue opportunities.

## Phase 7: analytics and SEO growth

Add analytics, structured SEO iteration, reporting, and growth experiments.
